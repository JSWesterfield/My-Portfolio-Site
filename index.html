College - Past

Leaving high school I enlisted in the United States Navy as an Information System Technician, learning bits of Linux and C++ programming. Leaving as a Lead Information Systems Technician 3rd class, enrolling in Northern Illinois University majoring in Economics, learning statistical data research, I eventually began teaching myself bits of R and Python programming.

Software Engineer - Current

Love of code and building new software currently describes my life. I have developed software in a multitude of platforms, frameworks and languages. Currently working at ArtFind, a social gaming web application as a software developer, building a crytocurrency with Raxxcoin, developing a streaming content provider platform with Reality Junkies, I just love being involved in the software development process. Looking to one day become a legendary Full-Stack Developer and mythical Ninja Software Engineer, taking every application as a lesson to gain a step closer to mastery.
